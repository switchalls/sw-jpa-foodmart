Note that you can choose to open an IDE in offline or in-browser online mode. 
In offline mode you can clone the project locally and later push the changes online to submit your solution. 
You can switch between the IDE's offline and online modes during the test.    

To copy the given project and work locally, click "Start with Local IDE" in the browser when opening the test.   

See https://support.hackerrank.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000471247-Front-end-Back-end-Full-stack-IDE-FAQs for some frequently asked questions about the IDE.
